Food in China

There are so many traditional and special Chinese foods, according to the folk culture, district, religion, and festival. Here, introduce some special dishes, perhaps you have had it, or you have never heard

Tofu

Tofu is the most popular food in Chinese Society.It is also the main daily food in family

Dim Sum

Dim Sum is the most famous food in world-wide.It is a Guangdong Style snack which served as light meal.

Dumplings

Chinese dumpling is a delicious food. You can make a variety of Chinese dumplings using different fillings based on your taste and how various ingredients mixed together by you.
